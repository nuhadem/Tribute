The Life of Sheikh Muhammed Nayir Erzincan-i (k.s.). Shaykh Muhammed Maşuk Norşin-i (k.s.) caliphs, one of the caliphs of Sheikh Ahmed El Haznevi (k.s.) from the branch of the Tarikat-i Aliyya-i Nakşibendiyye-i Halidiyye, the half-century passed and still continues. carries out the irşad activity. Sheikh Muhammed Nayir Erzincani (k.s)

Important Events of Sheikh Muhammed Nayir Erzincani (k.s)

  • 1930 - He was born in 1930 in Çayırardı village / Gümüşhane.
  • 1934 - His family was settled in Çaykent village of Çayırlı district
  • 1939 - When he was five years old, he started to receive hafiz training from Oflu Fikri Hodja. He left this training unfinished due to a discomfort in his eyes. He was engaged in farming.
  • 1952 - Then he started to engage in science again. He took Arabic lessons. He settled in Istanbul
  • 1953 - He took Nuru'l-Izah from Abdullah Efendi and Qur'an training from Kesikbacak İsmail Efendi in Istanbul.
  • 1955 - He first received his Sufi education from Hacı Süleyman Efendi in Istanbul. He worked as a preacher in the summer until 1955 and studied science in Istanbul in the winter
  • 1956 - After his military service, he worked as an imam in Bayırbağ village
  • 1960 - After the death of his teacher, Süleyman Efendi, he became attached to Muhammed Maşuk Efendi. He received a license (qualification) from this person.
  • 1994 - His poems and mystical conversations were collected by Abdurrahman Tariktaroğlu in a work called Erzincanlı Muhammed Nayır: His Life and Ideas
